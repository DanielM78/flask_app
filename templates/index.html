About Career Concierge


"I no longer have interview anxiety," said a client of mine. That was when I knew I had a system that worked. What started as a handful of casual referrals from friends and relatives has turned into a holistic system for your college entrance and career transition needs.


At the start of 2017, I found myself looking for a way I could work from home. As a former recruiter, I knew what it would take to get me the job of my dreams. The question was, what was that job? What was the best way I could help people? Then the phone rang. It was a friend of mine and former client. She had a friend in need of resume and cover letter assistance. Over the next few days I worked with my new client. It was the best feeling when we went over her cover letter and resume for the final time. We were both so excited, and I remembered how much I enjoy bringing a personalized and fun approach to a task most of us would rather avoid. And so, Career Concierge was born.


Our Values
